Oxtail Recipe

This Oxtail Recipe is fall off the bone tender and full of amazing flavor. It can be made in about an hour with your pressure cooker or cooked low and slow in a slow cooker.

    Ingredients

  • Oxtails
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Scotch Bonnet or Habenero Peppers
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Power
  • Browning
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Ground All-Spice
  • Butter Beans
  • Dried Thyme
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Cornstarch
  • Beef Broth
  • Ketchup
